# Example Brief

## Topic

AI Agent adoption in small and medium businesses

## Audience

Small and medium business owners, operators, and department leads.

## Scope

Focus on practical use cases, implementation readiness, risks, and evaluation
criteria. Avoid speculative AGI claims.

## Source Policy

Prefer official documentation, government statistics, reputable industry
reports, and primary company materials. Treat blogs and social posts as context
only.

## Output

Chinese PPT support pack, 8-10 slides.
