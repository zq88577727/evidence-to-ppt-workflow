# ppt-master Input

## Objective

Create a concise 5-slide Chinese deck explaining the evidence-to-PPT workflow.

## Approved Slide Outline

Use `04_ppt_outline.md` exactly as the slide structure.

## Approved Claims

Use only accepted or needs-caveat claims from `03_claims_matrix.md`.

## Source Requirements

Every slide should preserve source IDs in notes or footnotes. Keep caveats visible where the claims matrix marks `needs caveat`.

## Style Brief

Use a clear workflow-oriented visual style. Prefer diagrams, gates, tables, and concise slide titles. Avoid exaggerated marketing claims.

## Hard Constraint

Do not introduce new factual claims not present in the approved materials.

不要新增未经审查事实。
